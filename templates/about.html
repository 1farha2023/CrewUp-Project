Our Story

Founded in 2020, CrewUp emerged from a simple observation: the influencer marketing landscape was fragmented, inefficient, and often failed to connect brands effectively with the right creators, while talented influencers couldn't connect with brands that aligned with their values.

We set out to change this by building a platform that prioritizes authentic relationships, data-driven insights, and measurable results. Today, CrewUp is trusted by thousands of brands and influencers worldwide.

Our journey is just beginning. We're constantly innovating to make influencer marketing more accessible, effective, and rewarding for everyone involved.
